About Libaas Mehal

A small boutique, run the way fabric deserves to be sold.

Libaas Mehal started as a one-woman effort to bring genuine Sundas, Bin Saeed and Hafiz Makhdoom lawn straight from Pakistan's summer collections to customers who'd rather shop from their phone than a crowded market. Every piece on this site is real stock we hold ourselves — what you see is what gets packed.

How ordering works

Every parcel from Libaas Mehal travels with the same four guarantees. Read them once, shop with confidence every time after.

01 — Open Parcel

Open before you pay

Riders allow open-parcel checking on delivery. Unwrap it, check the fabric and stitching, then pay — no surprises after the bag is sealed.

02 — Returns

Easy return & exchange

Wrong print, wrong shade, changed your mind — message us within 3 days of delivery and we'll arrange a return or exchange, no awkward questions.

03 — Cash on Delivery

COD across Pakistan

Pay the rider in cash when your parcel arrives. Available nationwide through our courier partners, no card or account needed.

04 — Advance Booking

Advance payment option

Prefer to book ahead or order from outside Pakistan? Pay a small advance via bank transfer or Easypaisa/JazzCash and we dispatch the same day.

What's in every 3-piece

All suits on this site are sold unstitched, exactly as photographed in our catalogue. The lawn is cut and printed, the embroidery is finished, but stitching to your size is done by your own tailor — this is standard for premium Pakistani lawn and lets every suit fit you, not a sample size.
